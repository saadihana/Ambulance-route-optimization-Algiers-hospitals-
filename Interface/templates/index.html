This is about efficiently directing ambulances

to the best hospitals for patients ensuring saving their lives.

One of the foundational algorithms we use is Breadth-First Search (BFS)

BFS explores all possible routes layer by layer, guaranteeing the shortest path in an unweighted graph.

To further refine the search, we employ Uniform Cost Search (UCS)

UCS prioritizes routes based on cumulative cost, ensuring that the chosen path is the shortest in terms of distance and the most cost-effective.

We incorporate heuristic-based algorithms like Hill Climbing and A*

By integrating these algorithms, our system ensures that ambulances are directed through the most efficient routes with real-time data.
